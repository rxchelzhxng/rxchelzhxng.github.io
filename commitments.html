Leadership and Volunteering

♡ Contributing to Society ♡

Waterloo Nanotechnology Conference Finance Director

Dedicated to providing unique insights from renowned speakers in the fields of nanotechnology, the WNC is inspiring the next generation of shapers in this field. As the Director of Finance, I liaised with industry partners and successfully acquired gold and silver tier sponsorships, budgeted conference expenses with Excel, and oversaw all finances. Despite the conference being shifted online, we attracted over 300 attendees globally and heard from 21 speakers to provide insights into 3 key areas of nanotechnology: health, information technology, and the environment.

Waterloo Engineering Student Ambassador

As an Engineering Ambassador on the Waterloo Engineering Student Ambassador Team, I am committed to give prospective students a personal and informative connection to Waterloo Engineering. Through volunteering as a guide at the Engineering Fall Open House, to speaking at virtual EngChats, I am proud to be sharing my experiences at Waterloo and giving back to our community.

Waterloo Stem Cell Club Executive

With the desire to help improve the quality of life of patients, I am honoured to be a part of the UW Stem Cell Club as an executive member. We work towards raising awareness about stem cell donation and recruit to match stem cell donors. As an executive, I recruited over 50 potential donors at our drive by educating campus students and staff. I learned to promote outreach events and plan the logistics to spread the ongoing need for stem cell donors.
